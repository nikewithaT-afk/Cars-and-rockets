Rockets vs Cars Ultimate

Aim: Survive or destroy the cars before they take out you or your NPCs!

Player Controls: Move: A/D | Jump: W | Shoot: Click toward cursor

NPCs: Start with 1 NPC. Buy more in the shop with points earned from destroying cars.

Cars: Move randomly and jump to hit the NPCs or player.

Cube = Player
Rectangle = Car
Small Cube = NPC
