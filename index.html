Rockets vs Cars — Floating Island

Aim: Survive or destroy the cars. Protect your NPC allies on the floating island!

Controls: Move: A / D — Jump: W — Shoot: Click toward the cursor

NPCs: Start with 1. Buy more in the shop (100 points each). NPCs wander the island and have 3 lives each.

Cars: Roam and jump up to the island to smash NPCs or the player. They have jump cooldowns.

Cube = Player
Rectangle = Car
Small Cube = NPC

Points: 0

Owned NPCs: 1

Score: 0 Player Lives: 3 Wave: 1 Time: 60
