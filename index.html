Rockets vs Cars

Aim of the Game: Destroy all cars using your rocket launcher!

Player: Move with A/D, Jump with W, Shoot with Arrow Keys + Space.

NPC Allies: Help you by shooting cars automatically, but fire slower.

Cars: Jump randomly and try to stay inside the arena. Avoid them!

Win Condition: Destroy all cars to win. Lose if your health reaches 0.

Score: 0 Player Health: 100
